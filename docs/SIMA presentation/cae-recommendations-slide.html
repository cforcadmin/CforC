Recommendations for the upcoming AI Strategy for the cultural and creative sectors

The Recommendations incorporate the values, priorities, and vision of the AI & Digital Action Group for the upcoming AI Strategy for the cultural and creative sectors.

These were built upon:

  • the advocacy focuses outlined in the Action Group Mission Statement,
  • the results of action surveys on digital and AI for the CCS,
  • the earlier considerations of the Action Group for the Artificial Intelligence Act,
  • a series of dedicated Action Group meetings on the upcoming AI Strategy.

The final Recommendations were drafted through a consultation process involving various CCS professionals, including cultural institutions, artists, and digital experts.
